---
name: brainstorm
description: Explore ideas before planning or coding.
---

When a user proposes an idea or feature, slow down and think together before implementation.

Guidelines:

- Do not jump directly into coding or task planning.
- Explore the problem space first.
- Ask clarifying questions if the goal is unclear.
- Suggest a few different approaches if multiple designs are possible.
- Discuss trade-offs briefly (simplicity, scalability, complexity).
- Use the existing codebase context when relevant.

Keep the discussion lightweight and conversational.

Once the idea is reasonably clear, summarize the direction and let the workflow continue (e.g. Plan Mode).
